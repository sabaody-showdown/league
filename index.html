About Us

Welcome to the Sabaody Showdown — a competitive One Piece Trading Card Game league featuring some of the best players in the european scene. We host nine matches a week, streamed live on Twitch and archived on YouTube with top-tier commentary.

League Format

This season features 4 divisions of 8 players each. Players face off in weekly matches, vying for a spot in the upper echelons. They also win a sealed box, and the top 2 players in each division swap places with the two that placed lower on the division immediatley higher.

Watch Live
