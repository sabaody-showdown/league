About Us

Welcome to the Sabaody Showdown — a competitive One Piece Trading Card Game league featuring some of the best players in the scene. We host three matches a week, streamed live on Twitch and archived on YouTube with top-tier commentary.

League Format

This season features 4 divisions of 8 players each. Players face off in weekly matches, vying for a spot in the championship playoffs. Expect high-level plays, deck innovation, and thrilling matchups every week.
